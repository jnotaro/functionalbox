General Questions

What is all about

Q: What is the main idea?

Answer

We want to build a social network where we help each other to build our OWN BOX. There will be companies giving special prices and conditions for our network. Next step, you publish your workouts, pictures of you box, invite friends to workout with you.

Q: Why do we use it?

Answer

Workout alone is for many people boring. But this is the future of fitness to build your own place to workout when you have time. You can also join a fitness center or personal trainer. You still can take profit of great fitness center structure AND also workout at home. Another important point is can signup functional box for FREE and you can take advantage of the discounts and special conditions that all types of companies in the fitness branch grant to our social network.

Q: What can I get here?

Answer

EVERYTHING.
Abos in diferent fitness centers, equipments, consulting to build your box... You can also sell your products.
As soon as we grow strong, we will be able to get more and more better prices, conditions and discounts.

Q: How can I start?

Answer

JUST SIGNUP.
We are in first phase of development. You can create your account and be ready for fun. We will be adding features weekly. The first promotions will come soon.

Features and Details

Q: Account is Locked?

Answer

Your account is visible for all registered members. We should be able to communicate, share question and articles. Secondly, we will dispose the albuns, Workouts feed and the promotions feed where different business grant us with discounts and/or special conditions. We will be able also to send requests to business after grouping by interest. For example, if there are 20 members wanting to buy a product. They group up, and send from here an email to the seller asking for special price/condition. These features will come as soon as we grow our network.
